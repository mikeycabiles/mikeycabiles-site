The Thesis

Personal branding is who you are. Personal marketing is how you grow it.

Personal branding is identity. Reputation. Point of view. Personal marketing is execution. Distribution. Systems. It is the practical side of getting your ideas in front of the right people on purpose.

AI-led personal marketing is the layer on top. It means using agentic AI as the operating team behind content, research, positioning, workflow, and offer development. Not as a glorified Google tab.

It's 2026. The world is moving to a fully AI and creator-led economy whether we like it or not. If you're not using agentic AI to market your personal brand by the end of this year, you'll be left behind.
